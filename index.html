Geek's Society

As our slogan says "Let's Widen our Ken".This society is just to enhance our knowledge and skills besides the academic enhancement.As to be a perfect engineer,we have to be technically sound other than just being into the academics.And this is the platform that embeds skills and techniques in the person and shape them to be a perfect engineer and make them technically sound in I.T sector.

This society was formed on 25th Septemeber,2018 after being approved the college faculty.They also understand that there is a need of something innovative within the college that would provide an interactive platform for sharing knowledge between students and reducing the gap between seniors and juniors. We are planning, After a great success we will try to expand this society to even higher standards.

"Hope we come out with flying colours"
